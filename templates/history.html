History of Formula 1

The key eras that shaped the sport.

1950

The Beginning

The Formula 1 World Championship officially begins, establishing a global top-tier motorsport series.

1980s

Turbo Era

Teams push engine technology to extreme levels, creating some of the most powerful racing cars ever built.

2000s

Ferrari Dominance

The early 2000s become one of the most iconic periods in F1, defined by consistency, speed and championship success.

2010s

Hybrid Revolution

The sport enters a new technical era with hybrid power units, energy recovery systems and advanced efficiency.

2020s

Modern F1

Formula 1 continues evolving with new regulations, closer racing goals and a growing international audience.
